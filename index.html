How to Play Cornerstones

🎯 Goal

Find all the cornerstone words hidden in the grid to win!

🎮 How to Play

  • Drag your finger or mouse to connect adjacent letters
  • Letters must be connected horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
  • Form words with 4 or more letters
  • You can backtrack by dragging back to the previous letter

📝 Word Types

  • Green words = Cornerstone words (find all to win!)
  • Blue words = Regular words (earn hints)

💡 Hints

  • Earn 1 hint for each regular word found
  • Spend hints to reveal letters, show word lengths, or get other help
  • Use hints strategically to find cornerstone words
